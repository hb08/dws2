Adoption Guidelines

Every potential adoptive parent must have the following qualifications:

  • Be willing and able to provide training, medical care and loving attention for a pet
  • Be 21 years old or older
  • Have a current photo ID showing age and current address
  • Proof of permission from Property Owner/Landlord for pets
  • If renting, Proof of paid pet deposit or that payment arrangements have been made
  • Pay adoption fees
  • Permission of all adults living at the residence
  • Other animals residing with you must be current on vaccinations and dogs should be current on heartworm prevention

All adopted dogs will be spayed/neutered, microchiped, and up to date on shots and heartwork medicine.

Success Story

Blitz

Blitz has a forever family with Candice. Discovered in a storm drain with a broken leg, and severely underweight, Blitz proved to be a sweet little girl. It was love at first site for her new owner, Candice. After six months of working with Candice, Blitz has been certified as a therapy dog!
