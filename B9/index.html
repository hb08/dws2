Welcome!

House Your Bunny Feed Your Bunny Understand Bunny Play With Bunny

Owning a pet rabbit can be quite the experience. Surprisingly intelligent, loving, and even more adorable than you can imagine, it can be a bit overwhelming to know where to start preparing for your first bunny, or what to do once they are home.

Housing your rabbit is a great place to start, from finding the right cage to bunny proofing are a great way to make sure your new friend feels like they belong.

Finding the right food for your bunny is equally important. Treats are always a great idea. Just make sure you check out our list of toxic foods, so a gift to your fuzzy friend doesn't end up hurting them!

Keeping your bunny entertained is always a great way to prevent some bad behavior. Toys are a wonderful place to start. There are plenty of store bought toys to choose from. For those with a little time and imagination, making your own can be a less expensive and just as fun alternative!

Once your new friend is home, deciphering bunny body language can be a little difficult. Bunnies are very different from cats and dogs! They make different noises, and like to play and cuddle in their own unique way.

So if you are looking for information on a possible new pet, or are wanting to find out more about the little bunny who already has a place in your heart, this is a great starting point. If you have any questions, comments, or suggestions, feel free to contact me. Please keep in mind: I am not a veterinarian or medical professional. If you find yourself in need of one, please contact one near you and get your bunny seen ASAP!
