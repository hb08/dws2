Helpful Links

In my search for bunny knowledge, I've come across some helpful sites. Below are the best of what I've found.

  • Information on Everything
    • House Rabbit Society

      Information on Anything You Want to Know

    • My House Rabbit

      A good site to start with.

    • Too Many Bunnies

      A non-profit bunny rescue with lots of information.

    • Sacramento House Rabbit Society

      Another non-profit site with days worth of information.

  • Behavior
    • Bunny Hugga

      An amazingly extensive and well organized list of what your bunny is doing, and anything else you may need or want to know.

    • Rabbit Wise

      A breakdown of bunny behavior.

    • Fuzzy Rabbit

      An easy to read FAQ of bunny behavior both cute and disgusting.

  • Bunny Proofing
    • Bunny Proof

      Step by step guides on bunny proofing, and a neat list on toys.

    • Pet Education

      An article on bunny proofing from a site with tons of articles on a variety of animals.

  • Toxic Foods
    • Wisconsin House Rabbit Society

      A PDF of various levels of poisonous plants.

    • Rabbit Matters

      An easy to understand guide to foods you can and cannot feed a rabbit, along with lots of useful information.

    • Small Animal Channel Message Board

      A user contributed list of bad foods.

  • Items
    • Pet Werks

      A store for toys, homes, and everything else you may need.

    • Bunny Approved

      Colorful and easy homemade toy guides, and plenty of bunny paraphernalia from toys, food, and even clothing you can wear.

    • Curious Bunny

      A few step by step guides on how to build toys, and spreadsheets for the super organized.

    • Happy Rabbit Toys

      A place to buy fully assembled toys, or hard to find parts to make your own. (US only)
